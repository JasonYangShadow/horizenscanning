A policy tool for horizon scanning to improve strategic planning in urban areas

This tool will systematically collect a broad range of data to identify, assess, and cluster emerging urban issues. With the data collected we plan to structure emerging trends in different STEEP categories(social, technological, economic,ecological, and political) and index them based on their uncertainty and potential impact on urban metabolism
